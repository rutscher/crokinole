Key trade-offs

  • A gives the biggest board but scatters info across both sides
  • B cleanly separates score info (top) from board interaction (below) — easiest to scan
  • C maximizes board at the expense of score readability (floating overlay)

My recommendation: B — the score header keeps info readable while the board stays large. The clear separation also works well with the rotated Player 1 half.
