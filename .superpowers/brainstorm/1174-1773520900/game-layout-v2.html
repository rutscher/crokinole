Rotated Split

Phone lays flat on the table. Rob's half is flipped 180° so he can read it from across the board. Dave's half reads normally from his side. Each player taps their own ring buttons to score their discs. The center bar has shared controls (End Round, Undo).

How it works in practice

1. Phone sits flat between players on the table
2. Each player's name, score, and ring buttons face them
3. After all discs are shot, either player taps End Round in the center
4. App calculates the difference and adds to the leader's game score
5. Hammer indicator moves to the other player
