Summary of changes

  • Score bar: Opposing progress bar with integrated dual scores (your score bold, opponent muted). Shows committed game score only.
  • Center bar: Score bar + tiny menu/R# row. No End Round button.
  • Swipe right: Confirms "I'm done recording." Board dims, lock icon overlay appears.
  • Swipe left: Un-confirms. Board becomes interactive again.
  • Both confirmed: Round auto-ends, difference-based scoring calculates.
  • End Round: Moved to menu for early-end edge cases.
  • Undo button: Removed. Tap-to-remove handles disc corrections. Swipe-left unlocks confirmed state.
