C: Scores at Leading Edge

Numbers sit at the tip of each fill, moving with the bar as the score grows. The score literally shows where each player is in the race. P1's "42" rotated at the left fill edge.

My recommendation: B (Flanking) — always readable regardless of fill level (a score of "5" on option A or C would be cramped inside a tiny fill). The color-matched numbers flanking the bar keep it clean and the rotated P1 score is easy to read from across the table.
