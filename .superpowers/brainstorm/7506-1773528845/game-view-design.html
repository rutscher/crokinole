Key design decisions in this mockup

  • Wood rail — thin birch band at top, bottom, and flanking the center bar. Frame, not decoration.
  • Playing surface — warm espresso (#1f1b17) with subtle vignette and inner shadow from the rail.
  • Ring buttons — same colors, now with radial gradient lighting (top-left light source) making them feel dimensional like discs.
  • Center bar — darker than the surface, bounded by wood rails. "End Round" picks up the birch/gold tone.
  • Leading player — green score tint (kept from current design, it works).
  • No blue/red gradients — replaced with the neutral warm surface. Player identity comes from position, not background color.
